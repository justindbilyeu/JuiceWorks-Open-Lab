## The Thesis

Permalink: The Thesis

Information is everything.

Not metaphorically. Literally. At the deepest level we can currently probe, reality appears to be the structure of information flow — geometry, forces, matter, consciousness, all of it downstream of that substrate.

And information has been gatekept. By institutions. By credentials. By the idea that serious science requires expensive equipment, prestigious affiliations, and the right kind of degree.

That idea is wrong. And this lab exists to prove it.

An intelligent person with an iPhone, a rigorous methodology, and a distributed AI research team can do real science. Can ask hard questions. Can produce falsifiable conjectures, run computations, and advance human understanding.

Everything in this lab is evidence of that. Built from a phone. In public. Under open-source licenses.

The map is here. Each repo is territory.

### Layer 2 — The Computation

Permalink: Layer 2 — The Computation

_Running the conjectures against reality_

**AutKompute** *(Archived — paused pending RG/GUES restart)*
The first place GUES can fail. Computes Aut(K) for finite constraint systems. Seven models run across two rounds. First non-abelian group (S3) produced from a single K via orientation reversal — without construction. Random systems produce trivial automorphisms. Structured systems don't. The conjecture is still alive.

**ITPU** *(Archived — paused pending downstream need)*
Information-Theoretic Processing Unit. Computes entropy, mutual information, k-NN estimators. Hardware-ready architecture. The measurement instrument for the federated universe hypothesis — when AutKompute produces two orbit sectors, ITPU measures whether they're independently specifiable or necessarily coupled.

## The Workflow

Permalink: The Workflow

How information flows through this lab:

```
INTUITION (ResonanceGeometry)
    ↓
FORMALIZATION (Resonance_Geometry, GUES)
    ↓
ADVERSARIAL REVIEW (justasking → distributed AI team)
    ↓
COMPUTATION (AutKompute, ITPU)
    ↓
METHODOLOGY GATE (The-Charter, Resonance-Method)
    ↓
EXPERIMENT BUNDLE (Resonance-Engine)
    ↓
PREREGISTERED TEST (Fold-Engine-Intrinsics)
    ↓
PUBLIC RECORD (iQueue)
    ↓
NEXT QUESTION
```

Each step is a constraint on what survives. Most ideas don't make it through. That's the point.

The ideas that do survive are worth something.

## The Thesis

Information is everything.

Not metaphorically. Literally. At the deepest level we can currently probe, reality appears to be the structure of information flow — geometry, forces, matter, consciousness, all of it downstream of that substrate.

And information has been gatekept. By institutions. By credentials. By the idea that serious science requires expensive equipment, prestigious affiliations, and the right kind of degree.

That idea is wrong. And this lab exists to prove it.

An intelligent person with an iPhone, a rigorous methodology, and a distributed AI research team can do real science. Can ask hard questions. Can produce falsifiable conjectures, run computations, and advance human understanding.

Everything in this lab is evidence of that. Built from a phone. In public. Under open-source licenses.

The map is here. Each repo is territory.

## The Research Stack

This is not a collection of disconnected projects. It is a single research program operating at multiple scales simultaneously — from the philosophical foundations of reality to the practical infrastructure of democratized science.

### Layer 0 — The Question

*What is reality made of at the deepest level?*

**[ResonanceGeometry](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/ResonanceGeometry)**
The origin. Raw intuition, experiential geometry, the feeling that drove everything that followed. A hyperpixelated fractal fabric underlying perception. This is where it started — before formalism, before rigor, before the question had words.

### Layer 1 — The Physics

*Formalizing the intuition into testable structure*

**[Resonance_Geometry](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/Resonance_Geometry)**
The rigorous twin. Dynamical systems, running code, empirical results. RFO Stability Wedge, Toy Universe with Kuramoto oscillators, Non-Hopf transitions. This is where the intuition first compiled into something measurable.

**[Geometric-Plasticity-](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/Geometric-Plasticity-)**
Empirical grounding. Phase maps, ringing thresholds, hysteresis resonance. The α–η phase diagram with K contours. Results matching engineering-rule baselines to 1–2%. Real data, defensive disclosure, timestamped.

**[GUES](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/GUES)**
The current frontier conjecture. Geometric Unification through Emergent Structure — an educated guess, rigorously structured. Constraint structure K = (X, A) as the primitive object. Everything — distinction, information, causality, geometry, gauge freedom — emerges from it in that order. Evidence Level E1.

**[geometric-unity-gate1](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/geometric-unity-gate1)**
Prior work on the ambient object question. Full derivation of the Shiab operator for Weinstein’s Geometric Unity — unique up to a scalar, coefficient −1/2. Gate 1: CLOSED PASS. Produced by distributed AI research team under the Charter.

### Layer 2 — The Computation

*Running the conjectures against reality*

**[AutKompute](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/AutKompute)**
The first place GUES can fail. Computes Aut(K) for finite constraint systems. Seven models run across two rounds. First non-abelian group (S3) produced from a single K via orientation reversal — without construction. Random systems produce trivial automorphisms. Structured systems don’t. The conjecture is still alive.

**[ITPU](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/ITPU)**
Information-Theoretic Processing Unit. Computes entropy, mutual information, k-NN estimators. Hardware-ready architecture. The measurement instrument for the federated universe hypothesis — when AutKompute produces two orbit sectors, ITPU measures whether they’re independently specifiable or necessarily coupled.

### Layer 3 — The Methodology

*Keeping the work honest*

**[The-Charter](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/The-Charter)**
Research Assistant Charter v2.7. The governing methodology for all empirical claims in this lab. No claims without tests. No tests without thresholds. No thresholds without numbers. No numbers without a run. Gate protocol, evidence hierarchy, coherence controller, productive skepticism protocol.

**[Resonance-Method](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/Resonance-Method)**
The broader philosophical frame. Distributed cognition, constraint-driven convergence, emergence through adversarial pressure. RM-01/02/03 standards. The methodology layer that makes multi-AI collaboration produce science rather than consensus.

### Layer 4 — The Infrastructure

*Tools that make the work executable*

**[Resonance-Engine](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/Resonance-Engine)**
Discovery compiler + coherence controller. Turns research questions into preregistered experiment bundles — CLAIM, NULLS, PREREG, COHERENCE_METRICS. Hard gates enforcing numeric thresholds. Bundle 0001 validates the RFO Ringing Wedge from Resonance_Geometry end-to-end.

**[justasking](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/justasking)**
Multi-LLM fan-out tool. One idea → tuned prompts for Claude, Sage, Gemini, DeepSeek, Grok → synthesized brief. The practical implementation of the distributed AI research team. Information-geometry lens baked into prompt generation. GitHub Actions trigger or live UI via Cloudflare Worker.

**[Fold-Engine-Intrinsics](https://github.com/justindbilyeu/Fold-Engine-Intrinsics)**
Preregistered black-box LLM evaluation suite. Paraphrase invariance, serving determinism, context robustness, self-consistency, order invariance. Pilot-calibrated thresholds, cryptographic hashes, full reproducibility. The preregistration scaffold for Test C of the federated universe hypothesis.

## The Workflow

How information flows through this lab:

```
INTUITION (ResonanceGeometry)
    ↓
FORMALIZATION (Resonance_Geometry, GUES)
    ↓
ADVERSARIAL REVIEW (justasking → distributed AI team)
    ↓
COMPUTATION (AutKompute, ITPU)
    ↓
METHODOLOGY GATE (The-Charter, Resonance-Method)
    ↓
EXPERIMENT BUNDLE (Resonance-Engine)
    ↓
PREREGISTERED TEST (Fold-Engine-Intrinsics)
    ↓
PUBLIC RECORD (iQueue)
    ↓
NEXT QUESTION
```

Each step is a constraint on what survives. Most ideas don’t make it through. That’s the point.

The ideas that do survive are worth something.
